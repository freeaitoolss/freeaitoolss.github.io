Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI really replace hiring staff?

For writing, design, and basic customer service — largely yes. ChatGPT can draft content that would take a junior copywriter an hour, in 30 seconds.

Are these tools secure for business use?

Generally yes. Google Analytics, Mailchimp, and Canva are enterprise-grade tools trusted by millions of businesses. For ChatGPT, avoid pasting sensitive customer data — opt out of data training in settings.

What's the first tool I should try?

Start with ChatGPT. Ask it to write your next customer email. The speed and quality will immediately show you how powerful these free tools are.
