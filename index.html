Our Process

How I Review Every Tool

Every review follows the same process — so you can trust the results.

01
Sign Up & Test Free Tier
I only sign up for the free plan — never a paid trial. I test what you actually get for free, not what you'd get if you paid.
02
Real Tasks, Not Toy Demos
I use each tool on real writing jobs, real images, real business tasks. If it breaks or is too limited, I say so — honestly.
03
Compare Against Alternatives
Every tool is compared to at least 3 alternatives. I only recommend a tool if it's genuinely one of the best options for free users.
04
Updated Every 3 Months
AI tools change fast. I revisit every review quarterly to update limits, features, and rankings so you always have current info.
