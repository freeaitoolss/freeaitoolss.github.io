The creator stack: ChatGPT (writing) + Canva (design) + CapCut (video) + Descript (podcasts) + Ubersuggest (SEO). All free. All essential.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make money from content created with free AI tools?

Absolutely. There are no restrictions on monetising content made with these tools. Thousands of creators earn full-time incomes using this exact free stack.

Will AI content get penalised by Google?

Google's policy targets low-quality, unhelpful content — not AI-written content specifically. Well-researched, genuinely useful articles written with AI assistance rank normally. Always edit AI output to add your perspective.

What's missing from free tools that paid would add?

Higher usage limits, background removal (Canva Pro), more transcription hours (Descript), and unlimited SEO searches. For most solo creators, the free limits are sufficient to build a serious content business.
